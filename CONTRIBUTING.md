# Contributing to CHITTA

We welcome contributions to the CHITTA substrate! Whether you are fixing bugs, improving documentation, or proposing new hierarchical reasoning patterns, your help is appreciated.

## How to Contribute

1.  **Fork the repository** on GitHub.
2.  **Create a branch** for your feature or fix.
3.  **Submit a Pull Request** (PR) with a clear description of your changes.

## Areas for Improvement

*   **FFP Optimization:** Improving the efficiency of Fibonacci fractal pyramids.
*   **Reflexive Brain Tuning:** Fine-tuning the 135M model for faster response times.
*   **Documentation:** Expanding the research paper and technical guides.

## Code of Conduct

Please be respectful and professional in all interactions. Our goal is to build a collaborative environment for the evolution of AI.
